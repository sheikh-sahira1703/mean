Demerits of mean

Here are the demerits of using mean as a descriptive statistic for calculations

  1. Affected by outliers so a single outlier can increase the mean significantly hence it does not give you an accurate idea about the data
  2. Can’t be calculated for open ended intervals (eg below 40 or above 60)
  3. Can’t be calculated for NA values

    4. Mean is a good descriptive statistic to replace the missing values with it in QUANTITATIVE datasets
